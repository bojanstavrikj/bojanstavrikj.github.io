About

I am originally from Macedonia, where I grew up until I was 12. Afterwards I moved to Budapest, Hungary; where I would spend the next 8 years. I finished my Highschool there and went off to Rotterdam to do my bachelor degree in Economics. Next I started working in Citibank in Budapest.

Right now I am living and doing my masters degree in Lisbon I am a master student at NOVA IMS in Data Science and Advanced Analytics with a major in Business Analytics. I am currently looking for entry-level/internship job opportunities in the area of data science. I have experience with supervised/unsupervised machine learning models using Python. I would be interested to start at any time and most importantly I'm looking for an opportunity to do a master thesis while working on a real work project.
